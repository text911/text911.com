Why text-to-911 important

First things first: The best way to reach emergency services is still to call them, even if your area supports texting 911.

But not everyone can call 911, especially in emergency situations. For someone who is deaf or hard of hearing, or who has a speech disability, a voice call might be difficult or unclear, and calling a video interpreter can take several minutes that the caller doesn‚Äôt have. Someone could be in a situation where a voice call would place them in further danger, like domestic abuse or a break-in. It could be as simple as not being able to speak or understand English easily.

For many, texting 911 is as essential as 911 itself.
