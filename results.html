Can you text 911 in Redwood City, CA?

Yes

Texting 911 has been possible since 2014, but still “most consumers cannot reach 911 by sending a text message”, according to the FCC. The major cell carriers all support it, but many of the over 6,000 local 911 centers haven’t been able to upgrade.

Text911 is an open source project made by Michael and Dave.

We are not affiliated with the U.S. Government or the FCC. The best way to reach emergency services is still by calling 911. Contact your local jurisidciction for the most up-to-date information. Text911 only provides results for the United States.
